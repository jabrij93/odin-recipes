Maggi Goreng

Description

Fried Maggi is a simple and quick to be prepared if you're lazy to go to Mamak Restaurant. We'll be using Curry Maggi Noodle. In this recipe, I am going to add vegetables according to what I have in my refrigerator but be free to add according to your own preferences.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of Curry Maggi Noodle with it's paste
  • 1 crushed garlic
  • 1 egg
  • 2-3 prawns
  • A piece of curdled soybean milk, sliced thin
  • Cabbage
  • Mustard
  • Chilli
  • Chilli sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • Thin lime

Steps

  1. Stew the semi-cooked maggi, make sure that it is not fluffy and then toss.
  2. Heat oil until the sliced garlic turned yellowish.
  3. Break eggs and spread the maggi paste onto the egg. Mixed it well and add prawns.
  4. Add maggi, fried curdled soybean milk, cabbage, mustard and chillies.
  5. Add all the remaining maggi paste, add some soy sauce and chilli sauce until it is fully flavour. Mixed it all well.
  6. Finished and served with thin sliced lime.
